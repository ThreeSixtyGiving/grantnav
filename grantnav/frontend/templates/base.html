360Giving supports organisations to publish their grants data in an open, standardised way and helps people to understand and use the data to support decision-making and learning across the charitable giving sector.

Find out more about 360Giving

GrantNav uses open data published to the 360Giving Standard.

GrantNav enables people to search, filter and download across these datasets, for free.

For feedback and questions, please email grantnav@threesixtygiving.org
